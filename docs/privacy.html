ScreenFlow is designed with privacy as a core principle. All recordings, screenshots, and annotations are processed entirely on your device. Nothing is ever sent to any server.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information or user data.

  • ‚úì We do NOT require an account or login

2. How the Extension Works

ScreenFlow uses the following browser APIs to provide its functionality:

  • Screen Capture API: To record your screen, window, or tab when you explicitly request it
  • Tab Capture API: To capture screenshots of the visible area
  • Storage API: To temporarily store screenshot data between the capture and editor pages (stored locally only)
  • Downloads API: To save your recordings and screenshots to your computer

All processing happens locally in your browser. No data leaves your device.

3. Data Storage

ScreenFlow uses Chrome's local storage API only for:

  • Temporarily passing screenshot data from the capture to the editor (deleted after editing)
  • No persistent data is stored
  • No cloud storage is used

4. Third-Party Services

ScreenFlow does not integrate with any third-party services. We do not:

  • Send data to analytics services
  • Use advertising networks
  • Share data with any third parties
  • Connect to external servers

5. Permissions Explained

The extension requests the following permissions:

  • activeTab: Required to capture screenshots of the current tab
  • downloads: Required to save files to your computer
  • storage: Required to temporarily hold screenshot data locally
  • scripting: Required for full-page scroll capture feature
  • tabs: Required to open recorder/editor in new tabs
  • Host permissions: Required for full-page capture to work on any website

ScreenFlow does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13 years of age. Since we collect no data at all, this is not applicable.
